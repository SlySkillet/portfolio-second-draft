Simon Conrad

Hack Reactor software engineering student presenting just the beginning of my portfolio...

In my previous life I was a restaurant chef with 8 years of diverse experience in the industry. I began that journey as a bread baker and grew into restaurant cooking where I spent the bulk of my time on the saute station. Some highlights of my career were being sous chef at Momofuku Milk Bar, cooking for James Beard award winning chef Spike Gjerde and living and working in Big Sky, MT. Be sure to visit my recipe page for some more detail and tasty samples.

Currently, I am enrolled in Hack Reactor Software Engineering immersive and working away at building this portfolio out. I began this journey as a side project while I was working as a line cook at the Red Hen in Washington, DC, learning programming and web design basics from online resources. I’ve been curious about software design and its importance in the modern world. I want to contribute my skills to advancing our technological future!
